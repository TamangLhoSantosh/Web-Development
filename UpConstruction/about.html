About PCPS College

PCPS College is a multilingual, diverse, multicultural institution of higher education that ensures intellectual, research, and informed teaching. It is an integral part of the Lord Buddha Education Foundation (LBEF), Nepal's fi rst IT organization founded in 1998. PCPS College off ers International (UK) undergraduate and graduate programs in various fi elds of study, including Business, Hospitality Management, Information, and Technology with various opportunities to gain practical experience through internships, fi eld visits, and project work.

EXPLORE PCPS COLLEGE

Where is PCPS College?

We are located at Kupondole, Lalitpur. The distance between Kathmandu International airport and PCPS College is 7KM.

Is it approved by Ministry of Education, Nepal Government & recognized by TU?

Yes, PCPS College has been duly approved by Ministry of Education, Nepal Government to run the courses in partnership with University of Bedfordshire, UK. Both the courses are duly recognised by Tribhuvan University too.

Is it a new institution?

PCPS College was established in 2012 and started operation from 2017 after the approval from MOE. Lord Buddha Education Foundation (LBEF CAMPUS) is the parent institution of PCPS and known also as The first IT College of Nepal.

Is University of Bedfordshire a good university?

Yes, it is a world ranked university. As per the Times World University Rankings (2016-17 & 2017-18), University of Bedfordshire is ranked 601-800. University of Bedfordshire is an excellent university which has the heritage of more than 100 years.
